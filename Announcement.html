Wildfire

"Chosen, Created, Called: A Purpose Driven Profession"

The young leaders/professionals of our church have instilled the Word of God in the recently graduated students of Colegio de Amore, uniting them in a gathering that highlights the significance of purpose in our lives, emphasizing how it brings meaning and fulfillment. For when God created us, He chose and called us according to His purpose. Our purpose can only be unveiled by the One who created us. And once we discover this God-given purpose, it will guide us to a truly fulfillling life.

General

Our only motivation in doing the ministry is to glorify God and honor Him with our obedience

As a cell church, we have a pattern to follow in doing the ministry as how it was modelled by the Apostles. Being intentional in preaching to win the city, making many disciples, strengthening and empowering every disciples in order to advance the ministry of God. As we were gathered tonight to build each other and challenged to have an open cell, let us all have the desire and be involve in doing the ministry to advance the Kingdom of God!

Great Light Youth

The boundaries established by God's Word are provided as our direction and protection in life, not to stifle what we find enjoyable. For He is more concerned with our eternity in heaven than with the pleasures this life on earth might provide. To learn more of God's boundaries in our life, come to our Great Light Youth Service at 7PM in Amore Gym. See you there!

Sunday Service

"Divinely Sculpted: Fulfilling God's Purpose with Unyielding Grace"

Just as God does not allow Joseph to abandon Mary because she conceived Jesus, He will not allow you to stay broken because you are following Him. Wait on the Lord, for even if we cannot see it, He is working for our good. So we must wait and believe.
