Miracles of Life

Ps. Emy Isaguirre

"Jesus performed many other signs in the presence of his disciples, which are not recorded in this book. But these are written that you may believe that Jesus is the Messiah, the Son of God, and that by believing you may have life in his name."

John 20:30-31

Dear brothers and sisters in Christ, today I want to talk to you about the miracles of life. God has given us many wonderful and amazing things to enjoy and appreciate, and one of the greatest miracles of all is the gift of life itself.

From the very beginning of creation, God has demonstrated His power and love through the miracles of life. He created the heavens and the earth, the sun and the stars, the plants and animals, and finally, He created us - human beings, in His own image.

But even after the creation of man, God continued to perform miracles to show His love and power. Jesus, the Son of God, performed many miracles during His time on earth. He healed the sick, fed the hungry, calmed the storms, and even raised the dead. Through these miracles, Jesus demonstrated His power and love, and showed that He is indeed the Son of God.

But the miracles of life do not end with Jesus. Every day, God continues to perform miracles in our lives. He gives us breath, food, shelter, and health. He blesses us with family and friends, and with the gift of salvation through Jesus Christ.

Furthermore, God also works miracles in the lives of others through us. He uses us to bring comfort to the hurting, hope to the discouraged, and healing to the sick. When we love and serve others, we become agents of God's love and power in the world.

So, let us not take the miracles of life for granted. Let us appreciate and give thanks for the gift of life, and for the many blessings that God has given us. Let us also seek to be vessels of God's love and power, sharing the good news of Jesus Christ and serving others in His name
