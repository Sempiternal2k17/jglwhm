Learn to live a life as a Christian

Being a Christian is often not easy and full of persecution. Frequently I hear the question, “Why is it so hard to be a Christian?”. Other people find it very easy to be a Christian. I tell people that honestly, though it isn’t easy for people to accept if your life as a Christian is easy for you, you likely aren’t living as a Christian should. In here we will teach the important things a Christian must do in their life.

What is Soulwinning?

Soul winning is preaching the word of God, giving someone an opportunity to give their lives to Christ and save them from going to hell.
