What is Wildfire?

Wildfire is a Young Adult Ministry is a specific area of ministry within a church that focuses on meeting the spiritual, social, and emotional needs of young adults, typically those between the ages of 18-35. It is designed to help young adults grow in their faith, develop deep relationships with other believers, and navigate the challenges and opportunities of this life stage.

The aim is to create a community that is supportive and nurturing, where young adults can connect with others who share their beliefs and values.
