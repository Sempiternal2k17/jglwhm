Spearhead

"Working season is not an obligation but a fulfilment of our life mission"

Spearhead is an Youth / Young Professional Ministry on our church designed to meet the unique spiritual and practical needs of working adults in the congregation. These individuals may be professionals in their fields or simply those who work outside the home and desire fellowship with others who are in a similar stage of life.

Mission

Spearhead's mission is to help members thrive in all areas of life, both personally and professionally, and to empower them to live out their faith in meaningful ways in the world around them.

Vision

Our vision is to create a vibrant and dynamic community of working adults who are living out their faith in every area of their lives.

Goals

The Goal of Spearhead is to help working adults become fully devoted followers of Christ who are living out their faith in every area of their lives, including their work, family, and community.
