Sunday Service

Welcome to our Sunday Service!

Our Sunday Service is a time for our church community to come together to celebrate God's love and grace. We gather each week to sing hymns and worship songs, pray together, hear a relevant and inspiring message from the Bible, and participate in communion.

Our worship services are designed to be welcoming and inclusive, and all are invited to join us regardless of background or experience with church. We strive to create a warm and friendly atmosphere where everyone feels valued and loved.

We would be honored to have you join us for our Sunday Worship Service and hope that you will find encouragement, inspiration, and community as we come together to worship God.

This year's theme

"Continue to move forward in one's faith journey, regardless of the obstacles or challenges that may arise."
