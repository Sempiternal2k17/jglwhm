Married for Life

Married for Life is a dedicated and organized effort within a religious or community setting that focuses on supporting and strengthening the marriages of couples. It is usually led by a married couple who serve as facilitators or mentors, providing guidance, counseling, and resources to help other married couples navigate the challenges and joys of married life.
