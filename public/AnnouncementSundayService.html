Sunday Service

"Divinely Sculpted: Fulfilling God's Purpose with Unyielding Grace"

Just as God does not allow Joseph to abandon Mary because she conceived Jesus, He will not allow you to stay broken because you are following Him. Wait on the Lord, for even if we cannot see it, He is working for our good. So we must wait and believe.

Sunday Service

"Embracing the Burden: Navigating Forward with a Broken Heart"

Healing starts when we stop to bleed. And the cure for a bleeding heart is forgiveness. Your past may have hurt you deeply, but it should not continue to do so. Decide not to bring all of your grudges against people and situations into this year.

Sunday Service

"Transformation through Ownership: The Power of Claiming"

Let us recognize that God is advancing us in maturity with each prayer that we ask. If we want to add something to our lives, God usually requires us to let go of some of our characteristics or habits that will not help us mature. If we want to be promoted, we must stop murmuring. If we want our relationship to be restored, we must let go of our pride. We must let go of our selfishness in order to serve others.
