Married for Life

What is Married for Life?

Married for Life is a program or initiative designed to support and strengthen the marriages of couples within the church community. The ministry typically offers various resources, programs, and events that provide education, encouragement, and fellowship for couples. This may include seminars, workshops, Bible studies, retreats, and social events. The ministry may also provide individual and couples counseling or mentoring services to help couples navigate challenges and grow in their relationship with each other and with God. The goal of a Married for Life is to help couples build strong, healthy, and thriving marriages that reflect God's design for marriage and positively impact their families, church, and community.

What is Marrige?

Marriage is a social and legal union between two individuals that creates a lifelong commitment to share their lives together. Marriage is a cultural and historical institution that has been recognized and practiced in societies around the world for centuries. In most cultures, marriage involves a formal ceremony or ritual where the couple publicly declares their commitment to each other and exchange vows, which may include promises of love, fidelity, and support.
