Welcome to our Sunday School!

Our Sunday Worship Service or also known as Sunday School is a time for our church community to come together to celebrate God's love and grace. We gather each week to sing hymns and worship songs, pray together, hear a relevant and inspiring message from the Bible, and participate in communion.
