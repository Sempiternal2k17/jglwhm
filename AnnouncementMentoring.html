2020 Vision Casting

We would like to take this chance to inform you that G12 Upland Cavite Mentoring Network has organized a “2020 Vision Casting”. This will be held on February 8, 2020 at 8:30AM to 12PM at King Bee Seafood Restaurant” Governor’s Drive, Manggahan, Newhall Commercial Center General Trias, Cavite. We intend to share with you our journey that has brought radical changed and growth in ministry as we learned to apply basic soul winning and discipleship principles as modeled by Jesus’ earthly ministry.

We will be limiting this invitation exclusively for “Senior Pastors & Spouse” ONLY. NO associate pastor is allowed. We would be greatly honored if you can spare sometime from your busy schedule to attend the gathering.

We are confident that the knowledge you will get out from taking part in the ‘2020 Vision” will be enriching and blessing at the same time. This invitation is limited for only “60 Participants” in a first come first serve basis. You may contact us through +639977396891to secure your slots.

We look forward with great anticipation of having you join with us. God bless you more!
