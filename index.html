"Our church is committed to teaching the truth of the Bible and providing a place of worship and fellowship where believers can grow in their faith and build relationships with one another."

Jesus the great Light World Ministry is a vibrant and welcoming community of believers that embraces the full gospel of Jesus Christ. We believe that salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ, and that through the power of the Holy Spirit, believers can experience healing, deliverance, and spiritual growth.

Whether you are a longtime believer or are just exploring faith for the first time, we welcome you to come and join us at Jesus the Great Light. We believe that God has a plan and purpose for every person's life, and we want to help you discover and fulfill that purpose as you grow in your relationship with Jesus Christ.

About Us

Vision

To win souls and make disciples, Soul winning is preaching the word of God, giving someone an opportunity to give their lives to Christ and save them from going to hell. Through this, the person can become a child of God, enjoy His goodness and inheritance, even while on earth.

Purpose

To love God and to love people, It's an undivided love. God is our priority. If we love God with all our hearts, souls, minds, and strength, then we won't allow other things to crowd in.

Goals

To make every believer a leader of leaders, As Christians, we carry the responsibility of representing Christ in the way we lead. It's not just for our sake but the sake of those around us.

Connects

Welcome to Jesus the Great Light, where we believe that everyone has a place and a purpose in God's family. Our church offers a range of dynamic ministries that cater to the needs of people of all ages and backgrounds.

Sunday Service

(For all ages)

Welcome to Sunday Service at Jesus the Great Light! We are excited to offer a place where children and adults can grow in their understanding of the Bible and deepen their relationship with Jesus Christ.

Wildfire

(Young Adults and Teenagers)

Wildfire is a dynamic and engaging program for teenagers and young adults who want to deepen their understanding of the Bible and grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ.

J12 Kids

(Toodlers and Children)

J12 Kids is a fun and engaging children's ministry program at JGLWHM that is dedicated to helping children grow in their faith and knowledge of Jesus Christ. We offer a safe and nurturing environment where kids can learn about God's love through age-appropriate Bible lessons, activities, and games.

Spearhead

(Young Proffesionals)

Spearhead is a supportive community at JGLWHM that is designed to help working young professionals grow in their faith and connect with other Christians who share similar life experiences. We understand that life can be hectic, and that's why we offer a range of programs and activities that cater to the needs of busy adults.

Married For Life

(Married Couples)

Married for Life is a dynamic and supportive community designed to help married couples strengthen their relationship and deepen their faith in God. We believe that marriage is a sacred union, and we are committed to providing resources and support to help couples grow in their love and commitment to each other.

Equipping

Jesus the Great Light Ministry also offers these programs that help any individual to grow in their personal and spiritual development. Our ministry is dedicated to providing guidance, support, and mentorship to individuals who are seeking to grow in their leadership skills, deepen their understanding of the Bible, and improve their personal and professional lives.

Mentoring

The Mentorship Program at JGLWHM is a unique opportunity for individuals to receive personalized guidance and support from experienced leaders in our church community.

Sol Class

Lifeclass

We believe that marriage is a sacred union, and we are committed to providing resources and support to help couples grow in their love and commitment to each other.
