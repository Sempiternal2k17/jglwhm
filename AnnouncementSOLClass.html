SOL Class

SOL Class is a comprehensive and inspiring class that equips individuals with the knowledge, skills, and spiritual foundation needed to become effective leaders within the Christian community and beyond. This transformative course is designed for aspiring Christian leaders who wish to make a positive impact on others, serve with humility, and lead with integrity
